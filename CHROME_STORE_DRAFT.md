# Chrome Web Store Draft: BugMind AI

## Extension Name
BugMind AI — Enterprise QA Intelligence

## Short Description (max 128 chars)
AI-powered QA assistant for generating, reviewing, and managing bug reports and test cases directly in Jira & Xray.

## Detailed Description
**Transform your Jira workflow with BugMind AI.**

BugMind AI is a production-grade enterprise assistant designed for modern QA teams, developers, and product owners. It integrates directly into your Jira Cloud or Server/DC interface to automate the most time-consuming parts of the bug reporting and test generation process.

### 🚀 Key Features
- **AI Bug Generation**: Automatically generate detailed, structured bug reports from any Jira story.
- **Smart Test Cases**: Create comprehensive test suites with one click, covering positive, negative, and edge-case scenarios.
- **Xray Integration**: Seamlessly publish test cases directly to Xray Cloud or Server, including manual steps and issue links.
- **Epic-Scale QA**: Process entire Epics in batch mode. Fetch child stories, generate tests for all of them, and run a cross-story risk audit.
- **BRD Analysis**: Upload or link a Business Requirement Document (BRD) to identify coverage gaps in your stories.
- **Security First**: Enterprise-grade PII redaction ensures sensitive data like emails, tokens, and IDs never leave your browser.

### 🛡️ Privacy & Security
BugMind AI is built with enterprise security at its core.
- **Local Redaction**: All data is scrubbed for PII (emails, JWTs, IDs) before being sent to our AI backend.
- **AES-GCM Encryption**: Your Jira and Xray credentials are encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-GCM.
- **Minimal Permissions**: We only request the permissions necessary to interact with your Jira instance.

### 💼 Team Collaboration
- **Shared Workspaces**: Work together in team-based workspaces with shared Jira/Xray connections.
- **QA Templates**: Standardize your bug reports and test cases with workspace-level templates.
- **Audit Logs**: Maintain a clear history of all AI generation events and workspace changes.

---
*Note: A BugMind AI account and a Jira instance (Cloud or Server/DC) are required.*
